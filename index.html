How It Works

Our Simple 4-Step Process

Step 1 — Submit Your Product Request

Tell us what you need and we take it from there. No complicated forms or long emails required.

  • Share a product link, photo, description or sample reference.
  • Tell us your target quantity, budget and preferred shipping method.
  • Our team receives your request and begins supplier research immediately.

Step 2 — We Source & Negotiate For You

Our sourcing agents locate verified suppliers and negotiate the best price on your behalf.

We compare multiple factories and wholesalers, verify their credentials and production capacity, then present you with a clear, transparent quotation that includes product cost, shipping estimates and our service fee — no hidden charges.

Step 3 — We Inspect, Pack & Prepare Your Shipment

Once your order is purchased and delivered to our warehouse, our team inspects every item carefully. We document the process with photos and videos sent directly to you, so you have full visibility before anything leaves China.

  • Thorough quality check against your specifications.
  • Secure, professional packaging to protect goods in transit.
  • Custom labelling and branding available on request.

Step 4 — We Ship Worldwide & Track Every Step

Your goods are shipped via your preferred method — air freight for speed or sea freight for bulk savings.

You receive a real-time tracking number and regular status updates through your dashboard, WhatsApp or email. We keep you informed from departure in China all the way to arrival at your address — whether you're in Accra, Lagos, Nairobi, London or anywhere in the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about sourcing from China or shipping internationally? We've answered the most common ones below. For anything else, reach out — our team responds fast.

How do I get started importing from China with OP International Sourcing Agency?

It's simple. Contact us via WhatsApp, email or our contact form with details of the product you want to import — a link, photo or description is enough. Our team will research suppliers, prepare a quotation and guide you through every step from purchase to delivery.

How do I know the products won't be fake or low quality?

We inspect every order at our China warehouse before shipment. Our team checks each item against your specifications and sends you photos and videos of the goods so you can approve before anything is shipped. We also verify suppliers before any order is placed.

What shipping methods are available and how long does shipping take?

We offer air freight (typically 5–10 business days) for fast delivery and sea freight (25–45 days) for large or heavy shipments at lower cost. We'll recommend the best option based on your goods, timeline and budget during the quotation stage.

Do you ship to Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya and other African countries?

Yes. We ship to Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, Senegal, South Africa and many other African countries, as well as Europe, North America and the Middle East. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, just ask — we likely do.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

There is no fixed minimum order from our side. However, different suppliers have their own MOQs, which can range from 1 unit to several dozen depending on the product. We'll always communicate the supplier's MOQ clearly in your quotation before you commit.
